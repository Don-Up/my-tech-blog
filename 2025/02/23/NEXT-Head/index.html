❌ Why Doesn’t <Head> Work in the App Router?

next/head does not work in the Next.js App Router (app/ directory). It is only supported in the Pages Router (pages/ directory).

In the App Router, metadata should be managed using:

  1. generateMetadata inside page.tsx (for dynamic metadata)
  2. head.tsx (for static metadata)
